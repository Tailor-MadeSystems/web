Web Services

Web Services are ways for applications to communicate over a network. You can keep your business logic hidden within your company but provide a way for vendors and clients to integrate with you by exposing a web service. Allow your clients to browse your inventory or give them a way to create bookings within your system. Expose your features as a web service and see how others integrate them within their applications.

  • Technologies:

  • REST
  • SOAP

Web Applications

A Web Application is a software running on a server which is accessed from anywhere anytime with an internet connection and a browser. Your custom application can provide each user his or her own role and previliges within the system. It can improve your descision making by providing data reporting. It can simplify data entry - preventing errors and duplications. Applications can do anything from fulfilling your work orders to processing batch orders during off hours.

  • Java
  • Cloud
  • Database
  • Cache
